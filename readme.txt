== Description ==

Create beautiful newsletters with our unique newsletter editor. Drag and drop images, text areas, buttons, social icons, posts and more. Add a signup widget to your website, manage your subscribers, and send fresh, modern newsletters all within your wordpress admin section.

We've spent over a year building this newsletter plugin and we're proud to finally release it. Sola Newsletters is still in BETA. Any feedback would be welcomed. While in Beta, we're also offering our premium version of Sola Newsletters for free to anyone who would be willing to help provide feedback.

= Features =
* Super easy to use drag and drop newsletter editor
* Send newsletters to 2500 subscribers
* Easy subscriber management
* Newsletter signup widget included
* Add your latest posts in your newsletter by simply dragging them in
* Our newsletters show up perfectly on desktops, notebooks, tablets and phones
* Beautiful newsletter theme included
* Add subscribers by either copying and pasting from Excel, or uploading a CSV file
* Export your subscribers to a CSV file
* Send your newsletters via Wordpress Mail, SMTP or Gmail

== Installation ==

1. Once activated, click the "Newsletters" link in your left navigation
2. Create a new campaign and follow the onscreen instructions


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I get the premium version? =

At the moment, the Sola Newsletters plugin is currently in Beta. This means that we are still trying to find and iron out any kinks in our code. The Sola Newsletter Premium version is available for FREE at this time. Simply go to http://solaplugins.com/plugins/sola-newsletters/ to get your copy.

= I've installed the plugin, now what? =

Once installed and activated, a link should appear in your left navigation panel within your wp-admin section. Click on the "Newsletters" link and follow the on screen instructions.
